Oatmeal

Description

Nothing fills you up and gives you the energy you need to start the day like a hearty bowl of piping-hot oatmeal. Oatmeal is my go-to breakfast because it is easy to prepare, healthy, and keeps me full until lunchtime. No pesky mid-morning cravings here! Let's see what you need to start making this your favorite breakfast, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 c. traditional oats (not quick-cook or steel cut for this recipe)
  • 1.5 c. water
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar (optional)
  • 1/3 c. blueberries (optinal)

Steps

  1. Add 1 c. tradiiona oats to a small pan
  2. Pour 1. c. water over the oats in pan
  3. Bring your pan to a boil over medium-high heat on your stovetop
  4. When your oats reach a boil, reduce heat to low and start a timer for 5 min.
  5. Stir oats as they continue to cook to avoid scalding and cook evenly
  6. When your timer goes off, transfer your cooked oats to a bowl and add brown sugar and/or bluberries to taste!
  7. That's it! You're done - enjoy!
